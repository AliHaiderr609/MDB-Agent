Before You Post - MDB FAQs

1) Is MDB graded or attendance-based?

MDB is for academic discussion only. It is not a graded or attendance activity.

2) What should I avoid posting?

Do not post non-academic messages like "Yes sir", "Done", "Present", "Good effort", etc.

3) Where can I find course pattern, syllabus, and next topic info?

Check the course announcements and uploaded handouts for official updates.

4) Can I share WhatsApp or external groups?

No. WhatsApp or external group sharing is not allowed in MDB posts.

5) What happens if I post non-academic content?

The system filters such messages and provides an automatic guidance reply.
